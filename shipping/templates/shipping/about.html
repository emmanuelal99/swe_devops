Who We Are

Born to Connect
the World

Founded in 2026, in Greater Manchester, LogiTrack started as a small freight forwarding company with a single truck and a bold vision: make global shipping as easy as sending a local parcel.

Today, we operate in Europe, move over 5 thousand shipments per year, and employ 100 logistics professionals worldwide. But our founding principle hasn't changed — every shipment matters.

Our proprietary TrackSphere platform gives customers live visibility across air, sea, and road networks — delivering the confidence that comes from always knowing where your cargo is.
